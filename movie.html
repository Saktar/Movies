The Vista Theatre

Vista Theatre is a historic single-screen movie theater in Los Angeles, California, located in Los Feliz on the border with East Hollywood.

Address: 4473 Sunset Dr, Los Angeles, CA 90027
Opened: October 9, 1923
Capacity: 400

ArcLight Hollywood

The ArcLight Hollywood is a 15-screen multiplex located at 6360 Sunset Boulevard in Hollywood, California. All 15 screens feature stadium seating, and carry a THX certification for optimal sound and picture presentation.

Located in: The Dome Entertainment Centre
Address: 6360 Sunset Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90028
